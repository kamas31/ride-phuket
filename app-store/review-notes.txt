APP STORE REVIEW NOTES — Ride Phuket
====================================

WHAT THIS APP IS
Ride Phuket is a scooter rental MARKETPLACE/DIRECTORY for Phuket, Thailand.
It is NOT a booking platform. It does NOT process payments.
It does NOT facilitate in-app reservations.

The app helps riders find local rental shops and contact them directly via WhatsApp or phone.
All rental agreements are made directly between the rider and the shop.

KEY FLOWS TO TEST

1. BROWSE WITHOUT ACCOUNT
   - Open app → tap Explore
   - Browse scooters, filter by area/price
   - Tap any scooter → view details, photos, deposit info
   - Tap WhatsApp button → opens WhatsApp (external app)
   - No account required for browsing

2. SAVE A SCOOTER (requires account)
   - Tap the heart/save icon on any scooter
   - App prompts sign-in if not authenticated
   - After sign-in: saved scooters appear in Saved tab

3. CONTACT A SHOP
   - Tap "Message on WhatsApp" on any scooter detail page
   - Opens WhatsApp with pre-filled message to the shop
   - This is a direct external link — Ride Phuket does not intermediate

4. PARTNER DASHBOARD (shop owner account)
   - Sign in with shop owner account
   - Tap Partner in navigation
   - Dashboard shows fleet, analytics, and inquiry activity
   - Toggle scooter availability in real time

NOTES FOR REVIEWER
- The "Contact us to unlock" link in the analytics section opens an email to hello@ridephuket.com
  This is for partner plan upgrades — there is no in-app purchase flow
- WhatsApp links use standard wa.me deep links — requires WhatsApp installed on device
- If WhatsApp is not installed, the link opens in Safari (wa.me web fallback)
- Location data is not collected — the app shows area names (Patong, Kata, etc.) from shop listings
- The map feature uses Mapbox — no user location is requested or stored

IN-APP PURCHASES
None. This app has no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or payment flows.

ACCOUNT DELETION
Available at: Profile → Delete Account
This permanently deletes the user's account and saved data from Supabase.

SIGN-IN OPTIONS
- Email + Password (available on iOS app and web)
- Google OAuth (web only — not available in iOS app due to Google's WKWebView restriction)
