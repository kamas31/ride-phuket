Find the right scooter rental in Phuket and contact the shop directly — no booking fees, no platform charges, no middlemen.

Ride Phuket is a scooter rental marketplace for Phuket, Thailand. Browse verified local rental shops, compare scooters by price, location, and features, then reach out directly via WhatsApp or phone.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Browse scooters — filter by area, price, and features like delivery, helmet included, or deposit type
2. Tap any scooter to see full details — photos, daily/weekly/monthly rates, deposit rules, shop info
3. Contact the shop directly — one tap to open WhatsApp or call

NO BOOKING SYSTEM
There is no online payment, no in-app reservation, and no platform fees for riders. All rental terms are agreed directly between you and the shop.

FOR RIDERS
- Browse scooters across all Phuket areas: Patong, Kata, Karon, Rawai, Chalong, Nai Harn, Surin, Bang Tao, Phuket Town, and more
- Filter by price, delivery availability, helmet inclusion, deposit type
- See real photos, engine specs, and deposit requirements upfront
- Save favourites to your list
- Ask pre-contact questions and see public answers from shops

FOR RENTAL SHOPS (Partner Dashboard)
- List your fleet with photos, pricing, and availability
- Manage scooter availability in real time
- View inquiry activity and WhatsApp engagement
- Founding Partner program with advanced analytics

PRIVACY
Ride Phuket does not share personal data with third parties. Contact with shops happens directly via WhatsApp — Ride Phuket does not intercept or record these conversations.
